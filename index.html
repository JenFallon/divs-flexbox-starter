Hi, I'm Jenny

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who likes fun, clean, elegant code, with a pop of personality. When I work with people, I get to know them. Get a sense of their style, likes and dislikes, to get a full spectrum of the design and feel of the project.

I was born in Virginia, moved to Louisiana at age 15. I loved my teenage years spent in the amazing city of New Orleans! A city full of music & art stays with you forever.

By the time I went to college, I discovered by inner calling to want to travel. At the ripe age of 28, I became a white water raft guide in the Great Smoky Mountains of Tenessee.

At the age of 29, I found myself in Greece volunteering at the Macedonia/Greek border where 10,000 refugees were stuck. Not only was I fulfilling my need to help, I met my life partner.
